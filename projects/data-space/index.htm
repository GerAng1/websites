Data Space is a platform for the sharing, accessing, and long-term conservation of data produced by the Institute of Cultural Heritage Sciences. It allows access to innovative digital tools & services to increase the knowledge and enhance the conservation of the Cultural Heritage in an interdisciplinary perspective.
